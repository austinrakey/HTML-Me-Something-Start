Voidgazer: St. Louis' Premier Rock n' Roll Thugs

A sordid history of greasy bikers and nasty riffs.

After years of experimenting with the sound and finding the individuals with know-how, in 2020 Voidgazer assembled a crew of world-class riff warriors and readied themselves to write their deput LP "Dance of the Undesirables", an album made to reflect the crumbling malaise of the midwest, the blackened heart of America from whence they spawned. In their music as in life, they seek to ride the line of sleek, uncomprimising precision and total mental freakout. Now teamed with Reigning Phoenix Music/Atomic Fire Record's global vision, all cylinders are firing on full blast, and Voidgazer is armed to the teeth and ready to show the world some real heavy fucking metal.

Behold the indominitable might of our newest single, "Jesus Take the Needle"

"'Jesus Take the Needle' is a bleak tale of the messiah returning to modern day earth and failing his mission to save humanity, ultimately defaulting to living a miserable life of abject failure and drug use. It's a commentary on religious con-men and the bleak reality of life as the common man."
